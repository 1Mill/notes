## Building hightly effective teams

Highly effective teams are are rooted in self-organization and a container to focus collective outputs.
Containers build by leaders to provide compotent doers the clarity and control needed to discover and deliver great works.
Build the required mental models to build these hightly effectively teams by reading the below in order:

1. [Turn this ship around](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08V4TFFCK/?bestFormat=true&k=turn%20the%20ship%20around%20book)
2. [Leadership is langauge](https://www.amazon.com/Leadership-Language-Hidden-Power-Say/dp/B082XK834J?s=audible&sr=1-1)
3. [Thinking in Systems](https://www.amazon.com/Thinking-Systems-Donella-H-Meadows/dp/1603580557)
